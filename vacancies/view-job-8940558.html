Job Description:

Job Summary



  • Our Client, an international automotive giant, is seeking an IIB Developer possessing solid experience in IIB/ESB platforms.

  • They must have demonstrated experience working in multi-disciplinary design teams, Agile and/or XP development environments and have the capability to design and develop artifacts for the IIB/ESB platform.

  • They should have experience in connectivity and utilization of various platforms and transformation capabilities of the IIB/ESB.

  • The IIB Developer will leverage their deep experience in software engineering to ensure that deliverables meet ongoing performance and maintenance requirements.


Responsibilities



  • Design and develop interfaces and services for consumption and publishing for the landscape via ESB.

  • Develop Critical frameworks and perform proof-of-concepts in the integration platform

  • Work within an Agile development environment

  • Influence expert judgement and technical savviness when designing solutions

  • Responsible for integrating existing applications, web services, databases, and business process management systems in order to build advanced Enterprise Service Bus solutions


Qualifications
Education



  • BA/BS in Computer Science or related field is preferable. Equivalent combination of experience, education and training will be considered.


Experience



  • Minimum of 5 years of experience.

  • Experience with IBM integration technologies namely Message Broker, IIB, Data Power and WSRR.

  • The candidate should have knowledge and experience to utilize the connectivity, transformation, and mediation capabilities of IBM WebSphere Message Broker V6.x/7.x/8.x and IIB V9.

  • Solid experience in analyzing, designing, and deploying message models, message flows using the IBM WebSphere Message Broker Toolkit and IIB V9.

  • Must have experience in development, testing, debugging of flows via the IBM IIB V9 by using ESQL and other languages, nodes, graphical mapping tools, and pattern features.

  • Good experience in transforming messages with ESQL, Java and Maps and monitoring and performance of the IIB.

  • Familiar with concepts related to scalability, high availability, security and the general use of adapters in the IBM IIB /Message Broker environment.

  • Good understanding of MQ, Java, JMS, JDBC, XML, Xpath, SOAP, WSDL, Message sets and Message Parsers.

  • Prefer candidate who has knowledge of Datapower appliance, exposing stored procs elimination of CTG (CICS Transaction Gateway) and utilization of WSRR.

  • Ideal candidate must have migration experience of artifacts and flows from Message Broker to IIB.

  • Thorough knowledge of design patterns, Integration and Service oriented architecture patterns. Candidate should be familiar with SOA and Messaging Technologies.

  • Knowledge of event based integration, pub-sub, request/reply and synchronous/Asynchronous is mandatory.

  • Design service contracts, participate in JAD sessions with business and IT stakeholders, analyze requirements and participate in estimation activities.

  • Must have knowledge of UML and have the ability to create use cases, process flow diagrams, and functional specifications documents.
