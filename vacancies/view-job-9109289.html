GENERAL SUMMARY/ OVERVIEW STATEMENT: Summarize the nature and level of work performed.


The MGH Computational Pathology team innovates in the field of clinical genomics, digital pathology, and pathology informatics. We leverage software solutions and integrated data to improve the delivery of patient care. We are seeking a highly motivated bioinformatician with software engineering experience to work with our team of clinicians, pathologists, software engineers, and data scientists to employ and develop computational tools for the analysis of next-generation sequencing, clinical, and research data. The successful candidate will join a thriving computational pathology team to lead efforts in clinical genomics pipeline and algorithm development along with some opportunity to also work on digital pathology and pathology informatics.

Our Computational Pathology team innovates across multiple disciplines, including data science, bioinformatics, laboratory science, and software engineering. We are one of the first clinical laboratories developing and using machine learning models in routine clinical practice. We leverage the latest software tools to build data-driven integration systems and clinical decision support tools. You would be joining an experienced software development team that combines the best of industry and academia: we use agile software development practices and leverage the latest open-source libraries and tools for software deployment, data management, statistics, and machine learning.

As part of the Computational Pathology Team, you will develop innovative, data-driven solutions which directly impact patient care at MGH. If you are excited about the opportunity of making a real impact on patients through the development of information technology, we would love to hear from you!

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: Indicate key areas of responsibility, major job duties, special projects and key objectives for this position. These items should be evaluated throughout the year and included in the written annual evaluation.




  • Develop, validate, test, deploy, and maintain clinical and research genomics pipelines for analysis of: somatic variants (SNVs, indels, copy number for gene panels and exomes; gene fusions and expression for targeted RNA panels), genomic signatures (e.g., microsatellite instability, total mutation burden), germline variants (exome sequencing, low-pass whole human genome sequencing, copy number)

  • Develop/improve variant calling approaches (e.g., cell-free DNA analysis, minimal residual disease monitoring)

  • Develop/improve variant annotation approaches

  • Lead bioinformatics pipeline migration from on-premise to the cloud

  • Support bioinformatics needs for research projects where necessary

  • Support data engineering efforts to integrate genomics data with other clinical data

  • Where available, support software engineering and data science efforts in genomics, digital pathology, and healthcare analytics

SKILLS/ABILITIES/COMPETENCIES REQUIRED: Must be realistic, objective, measurable and related to essential functions of this job.




  • Significant experience in computer programming and proficiency in a general-purpose programming lanugage (Python, Java, Scala, C++ or equivalent, with Python strongly preferred)

  • Linux experience required

  • Experience working in a software team, including agile methodology, unit testing/continuous integration, refactoring, code reviews, version control and release engineering

  • Proven track record of delivering high-quality, production-grade software

  • Experience with bioinformatics tools preferred (BWA-MEM, Novoalign, GATK, Mutect, LoFreq, SAMtools, Picard Tools, bedtools, Variant Effect Predictor, Annovar)

  • Experience with developing bioinformatics pipelines (COSMOS, Galaxy, Terra, FireCloud, Common Workflow Language, Workflow Description Language)

  • Track record of architecting robust and scalable systems, including the design of internal and external APIs

  • Experience with databases (SQL, Postgres, MongoDB) a plus

  • Experience with Machine Learning (including deep learning) a plus

  • Experience with HTML/Javascript and popular libraries (jQuery, D3.js) a plus

  • Experience with full-stack development a plus

  • Experience with Docker/Kubernetes a plus

  • Experience with cloud computing a plus

  • Ability to work in a highly collaborative and intellectually challenging environment.

  • Excellent oral and written communication skills.
