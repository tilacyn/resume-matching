Your Opportunity


As an Oracle Developer you will design and develop software solutions to address gaps in core Oracle e-Business Suite capabilities across various application technologies (Java/OAF, PL/SQL, Workflow, etc.). In addition Lead/mentor other software developers including defining design and coding standards (exception handling, debugging, reusable code, etc.) and performing design and code reviews.


Your Key Responsibilities


Problem Solving



  • Analyze software specifications provide by Business Systems Analyst and translate them into technical solutions.

  • Troubleshoot and resolve defects to application problems.


Technical



  • Consult on the capabilities of application technology to identify opportunities to solve business problems.

  • Design and develop software solutions as per specifications.

  • Develop and execute unit test plans to ensure quality of software being released for integration and user testing.

  • Develop an appropriate production transition plan ensuring a smooth transition to production.

  • Establish design and coding standards for the team to follow.

  • Mentor other software developers assisting them in best practices and in solving problems.


Communication



  • Ability to communicate highly technical information to business users and analysts.

  • Communicate issues and solutions in a clear and concise manner.

  • Keep client, peers and managers informed on the status of issues.


    Project Management


  • Prepare time and effort estimates for assigned projects and tasks.

  • Given the tasks and scope of a project, successfully complete and document the project within the specified time frame.

  • Manage priorities across multiple projects simultaneously.

  • Plan, document, assign and implement production transition tasks to ensure a smooth deployment into our production environment.


Interpersonal Interaction



  • Interact with end user, peers, help desk, and management personnel to resolve issues.

  • Effective in interacting with users when designing, completing, and testing assignments.

  • Maintain a positive attitude when dealing with others in a pressured environment.


Your Capabilities and Credentials



  • Experience working with complex enterprise applications, preferably in an ERP environment such as Oracle e-Business Suite 11i/12 or Peoplesoft.

  • Experience in all aspects of software development lifecycle following best practice methodologies, preferably Rational Unified Process.

  • Experience working with one or more of the following application technologies, tools and frameworks: APEX, OAF, Oracle Workflow, JDeveloper, BPEL, SOA, and Web Services.


Education and Experience


At least 2 years of software development experience developing solutions using Java, SQL, and PL/SQL.


Typical office environment working with computers and remaining sedentary for long periods of time. Field work may include exposure to the elements including inclement weather.
