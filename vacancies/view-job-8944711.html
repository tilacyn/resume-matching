JOB DESCRIPTION SUMMARY



Business Intelligence Engineer


Sunbelt's Business Intelligence Engineer is empowered build the data infrastructure to support our team, so we can make it happen for our customers.


The Business Intelligence Engineer is responsible for the design, development, and implementation of BI data sets, reports, and dashboards. They will provide end user consultation and support for self-service BI as well as operational and managerial reporting. This role will work with a cross-functional team to ensure reporting needs are being met end-to-end.


Education or experience that prepares you for success:


  • Bachelor's degree preferred or equivalent work experience

  • 5 years of experience in Business Intelligence implementation projects

  • Application Development (Javascript, JQuery, HTML, CSS), Data Development (TSQL), Visualization Development (Cognos Analytics, SSRS Mobile Reports, SSRS) and Object Oriented Design (C#) experience

  • Engineering Development Methodologies (Waterfall, Agile, SCRUM, Kanban, Lean, etc.)

  • UI and UX design

  • Business Intelligence as it relates to visualization, data models, data flow, databases, and ETL




Knowledge/Skills/Abilities you may rely on:


  • High levels of activity, variety of conditions and constraints in a fast-paced environment

  • Change Management/Version Control (TFS, Git, Subversion, etc.)

  • ASP, JSP, PHP, Perl, MDX, R, Python, Tableau, Power BI, Excel Power View, Java, asp.net
