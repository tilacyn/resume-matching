Senior IT Developer - Retail Merchandising


The Save Mart Companies is one of the largest food and drug retailers in California and Nevada. It is home to a number of well-known brands in food retailing. You might have seen our banners that include Save Mart, Lucky, Lucky California and FoodMaxx. The Information Technology Department has an opening for a Developer in the Retail Merchandising technology portfolio. This position is located in Dublin or Modesto, California.


We could be looking for you! We are looking for dynamic transformational technology team members who can effectively incorporate their talent and passion to help us modernize our technology stack. The retail grocery business is exploding with new demands and opportunities. Our business partners are eager to upgrade their technology capabilities to meet the changing demands of our customers. These new team members will have an opportunity to influence the technical direction of our company. Job Summary:


The Developer role is a valuable team member helping to support and deliver the companies technology solutions. This role is first and foremost a developer but must be able to work with our business partners and vendors to understand new requirements and operational issues. They will design, implement and integrate new applications that align to the company strategies. Retail technology experience is a plus, but not required.


Key Accountabilities:



  • Evaluate, install, configure, and deploy new applications, systems software, products, and/or enhancements to existing applications

  • Ensure that applications meet business requirements and systems goals, fulfill end-user requirements

  • Collaborate with analysts, designers, and system owners in the development and testing of new software programs and applications

  • Design, develop, and install application enhancements and upgrades

  • Document system design and application support documentation

  • Identify and communicate system issues and presents potential resolutions to leadership

  • Integrate the proper use of security, logging and monitoring into the software development

  • Work with third party vendors to implement, integrate or troubleshoot issues

  • Champion and ensure compliance with organizational standards, practices and methodologies

  • Performs other duties as assigned


LOCATION: MODESTO, -OR- DUBLIN, CA.


Qualifications:



  • Ability to complete all phases of software development life cycle including analysis, design, development, testing, implementation, and support with minimal assistance

  • Writes applications that are legible, secure, maintainable, and well tested

  • Ability to build applications using Web Service Solution Stacks like LAMP and equivalents.

  • Ability to re-write applications from Ruby on Rails, .Net to Python, Java.

  • Understanding and experience with databases (Oracle, SQL Server, DB2, & MySQL) & stored procedures.

  • Experienced in Agile project methodology.

  • Proficient in two or more scripting languages (Python, Bash, JavaScript).

  • Firm understanding of System-as-a-Service (SaaS), Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), Public Clouds, Node.js & ReactJS.

  • Experience in developing and implementing systems interfaces, leveraging techniques such as SFTP, ESB, API integrations

  • Experience working with source control in a multi-contributor environment (Git, SVN, etc.), including understanding how to effectively utilize established workflows

  • Excellent analytical, planning and organization/time management skills

  • Experience providing technical leadership and mentorship

  • Good communication skills - written, verbal, interpersonal, and collaborative

  • Experience required in a multi-discipline project and troubleshooting environment
