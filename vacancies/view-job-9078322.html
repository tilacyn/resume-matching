Job Description:

The Applications Software Developer Intermediate will design, develop, and support administrative and self-service software applications, as well as build integrations between systems. This individual will be analyzing and developing solutions to address business requirements with a high level of reliability and execution speed.



Core Job Functions



Contributes in all phases of the project's life-cycle: planning and prototyping, design, development, conversion, testing and deployment. Provides guidance and training to less experienced programming staff. Works with functional team to determine operational feasibility and functional design by evaluating problem definitions and requirements. Applies applications programming concepts to design, develop, modify, debug and evaluate programs for a variety of functional areas. Adheres to project plans and change management procedures. Creates and executes unit test plans to ensure requirement specifications are met. Collaborates with the functional team in all aspects of development including end-to-end testing. Prepares technical documentation as well as process flow documents including diagrams, charts, etc. Adheres to University and unit-level policies and procedures and safeguards University assets.

Department Specific Functions

* Responsible for design, implementation, and maintenance of Identity and Access Management (IAM) enterprise-level solutions and integrations, ETL data feeds creation/monitoring, logs, system configurations, access policies, roles, automated provisioning and reconciliation of resources, components, testing.
* Responsible for producing detailed design documents to present the complete technology solution for a given implementation project.
* Plan independently the full range of actions needed to achieve desired end-results on complex problems.
* Document technical designs with charts, diagrams, schemas, and descriptive software component documentation.
* Research and advise on emerging technologies and with respect to industry best practices, procedures, and concepts.

* Design, develop, and implement technical components to provide Identity and Access Management (IAM) solutions for the enterprise, addressing authentication, authorization, identity provisioning, and audit.
* Troubleshoot and resolve system issues, review logs, debug production issues.
* Develop, test, and implement system changes and upgrades.
