Job Description:

The Information Technology Sr. Applications Software Developer will design, develop, and support administrative and self-service software applications, as well as build integrations between systems. This individual will be analyzing and developing solutions to address complex business requirements with a high level of reliability and execution speed.



Essential Position Functions
* Involved in all phases of the project's lifecycle: planning and prototyping, design, development, conversion, testing and deployment.
* Provides technical leadership to team members and management.
* Provides guidance and training to less experienced programming staff.
* Evaluates applicable industry best practices for possible adoption into our standards.
* Works with functional team to determine operational feasibility and functional design by evaluating problem definitions and requirements.
* Applies advanced applications programming concepts to design, develop, modify, debug and evaluate highly complex programs for a variety of functional areas.
* Adheres to project plans and change management procedures.
* Creates and executes unit test plans to ensure requirement specifications are met.
* Collaborates with the functional team in all aspects of development including end-to-end testing.
* Prepares technical documentation as well as process flow documents including diagrams, charts, etc.
* Adheres to institutional standard operating procedures.
* Keeps abreast of current industry technology trends.
* Keeps abreast of changes to product toolsets.
* Create and execute standard operating/work procedures.
* Seasoned professional who can work independently.
* Demonstrates a strong work ethic, is highly reliable and is willing to go the distance to get the job done.
* Has the ability to learn quickly on his/her own, is resourceful and is not intimidated by new technologies.



Knowledge, Skills, Abilities
* Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, both verbal and written.
* Highly developed problem solving skills.
* Broad experience with relational databases including knowledge of SQL.
* Self-motivated with a high commitment to excellence, collaboration and team achievement.
* Extensive experience and knowledge in systems analysis, development, testing and implementation of complex systems.
* Ability to work in a diverse, fast-paced environment.
* Effectively collaborate across technical and functional teams.
* Ability to juggle multiple projects and initiatives simultaneously.



Position Requirements



Bachelor's degree is required.
* A degree in Computer Science or Systems Engineering is preferred.
* 5+ years of related experience is required.
* An equivalent combination of education and work experience may substitute for a degree.



Career Ladder
The Career Ladder for the Sr. Applications Software Developer would be to move into the Software Architect or Manager, IT position.

Preferred Department Specifics
* Experience with ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) applications such as PeopleSoft.
* Experience working with Oracle Managed Cloud Services (OMCS).
* Experience in PeopleSoft - Fluid development using HTML 5, CSS 3, Java Script), Application Packages, Application Engine, SQR, Application Designer, PeopleCode, PUM, Security, and PeopleTools 8.56+. 
* Working knowledge of PeopleSoft Administration is preferred.
* Working knowledge of PeopleSoft Campus Solutions is preferred.
* Competency in an iPaaS tool such as Dell Boomi, Informatica, MuleSoft, SnapLogic, or an integration tool such as Pervasive, Jitterbit, etc. is a plus.
* Working knowledge of an object oriented language such as Java, C#, etc. and proven experience developing web applications.
* Familiar with Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) or web services such as SOAP and REST and related tools such as SoapUI, Postman, Oxygen, etc.
* Experienced in developing integrations that generate flat files or call APIs/web services to interface to Cloud platforms as a Service (PaaS).
