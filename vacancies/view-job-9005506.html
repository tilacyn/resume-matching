The Role!

The Data Warehouse Engineer will help develop data-centric solutions to meet the company's business's needs - specifically, integrating existing siloed data and architecting data solutions that reflect the integrated landscape. Working closely with the business and application teams including BI, the Data Warehouse Architect will analyze the business needs, and develop appropriate data deliverables, such as, data models, data flow diagrams, data dictionary and glossaries to meet them. The Data Warehouse Architect will also assist in issue resolution, in operationalizing Data Governance, in assessing, measuring and tracking data quality, and in creating and socializing data management artifacts, such as, SOP's, standards and guidelines, control processes etc.
This position will also assist in extending the scope of the enterprise data warehouse and analytics program.

What you will do!

The Data Warehouse Engineer's core responsibilities will include, but are not limited to:



  • Be a major contributor in the successful delivery of data warehouse design and implementation

  • Leverage experience in providing data warehouse solutions, including a strong background in SQL Development, ETL, and Reporting Tools

  • Research, analyze, design, and implement database solutions and changes

  • Transform complex customer business requirements into effective, efficient, and maintainable enterprise-level solutions


Provide input to project planning, requirements gathering, process recommendations, etc.



What it Takes!

Experience:



  • 5+ years professional experience as a SQL Developer or Data Engineer with knowledge of relational databases using Microsoft SQL Server, MySQL, or other RDBMS

  • Solid SQL Developer experience is a must; able to write complex store procedures, user-defined functions, common table expressions, and correlated sub-queries; able to optimize indexes; understanding of atomicity and transaction isolation levels

  • Good experience with ETL tools like SSIS; or experience with Informatica, DataStage, Pentaho, and other ETL tools is acceptable; experience with maintaining and enhancing load jobs



  • Some experience with Reporting tools is desirable, like Tableau, SSRS, QlikView, Sisense, Looker, PowerBI, SAS BI, Cognos, and others



  • Knowledge of best practices and principles for Data Warehousing, particularly the Kimball method

  • Experience or knowledge in Data Mining, Data Analytics, and Machine Learning is a plus

  • Cloud computing in Azure or AWS is a plus

  • Additional programming/scripting language knowledge is a plus: Windows Powershell, C#, Java, Python, R, JavaScript, PHP, Linux shells (ksh, bash)
