Job Description:

Role Description



  • Our Client is seeking a Java Software Design Architect.

  • As this is a long-term role, the ideal candidate is searching for longevity in the workplace and possesses a strong determination for the overall success of the company as an employee! The goal for the Architect is to provide a framework for the development of software that results in delivering a quality product released with new and enhanced capabilities to drive growth.


The architect will:



  • Collaborate with Product Management team to design software to meet business and technical needs

  • Create architectural approaches for software design and implementation to guide the development team

  • Analyze application requirements and technology approaches to ensure scalability

  • Act as the lead person on one or more software development projects, ensuring that the team is meeting technical and functional requirements

  • Encourage team members to think creatively and stay motivated, while keeping with the Company's development standards

  • Contribute to development of standards and practices for Java, JavaScript, and SQL development


Responsibilities



  • Work closely with the Product Management team to understand business requirements and end-user experiences

  • Analyze and help prioritize potential solutions and translate requirements into solid designs

  • Author designs that communicate how to intelligently and elegantly merge new functionality into the existing system

  • Refactor existing code where it makes sense, to manage technical debt, while avoiding unnecessary disruption to functionality that is already working, all with an eye towards optimizing performance

  • Use tools and methodologies to create user interface prototypes for the new or enhanced capabilities

  • Work with CTO and other architects to define all aspects of development standards from appropriate technology to coding standards and best practices for Java development

  • Help provide oversight, planning, and technical direction to the development team to ensure successful delivery of software projects

  • Lead the technical design and development, using object-oriented design techniques

  • Ensure that the software developed adheres to best practices and quality standards through code and design reviews

  • Work on multiple sub-projects simultaneously dealing with multiple stake holders

  • Additional duties as assigned


Qualifications
Education



  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field from an accredited college/university

  • Any certification is a plus


Experience



  • 10+ years of experience in Software Development with at least 3+ years of architectural work

  • Extensive experience with Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and UI design

  • Excellent knowledge of Data modeling and database design experience including strong SQL skills

  • Robust multi-tasking and strong communication skills verbal and written to articulate the architecture and respond instructively to questions about the architecture

  • Outstanding interpersonal skills, positive attitude, flexibility and a professional work ethic

  • Experience with presenting and articulating ideas to management

  • Interest in emerging technologies and innovation

  • Clear technological thinking and a sound understanding of technology solution design principles

  • Experience in systems analysis and design

  • Possesses a solid understanding of development, quality assurance and integration methodologies

  • Possesses the ability to multitask and juggle competing priorities

  • Understanding of Accounting principles preferred

  • The candidate should be self-motivated, and be comfortable functioning in a multi-tasking, tightly knit collaborative environment
