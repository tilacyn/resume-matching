Job Description:

Position Overview

Responsible for developing optimization solutions for routing and scheduling.  Provides strategic direction for functional area around engineering concepts, practices, and procedures to analyze, identify performance improvement opportunities, and/or implement technology systems that meet business objectives.

Job Responsibilities

  • Interfaces with stakeholders to identify relevant industry problems.
  • Analyzes and provides leadership with multi-disciplinary team of professionals to solve difficult industry problems.
  • Researches new technologies and/or monitors and evaluates existing technology for improvement opportunities to support technology life cycle.
  • Proposes efficient solution methodologies.
  • Supports and leads in the ongoing evolution and maintenance of solution methodologies.
  • Formulates solution approach and the underlying mathematical model to address needs defined by the Product Management team.
  • Owns the design, developing, testing, and tuning of models to ensure they meet expected performance and provide results that are acceptable to the business owner.
  • Works closely with Development teams to transfer technology and techniques to ensure the prototype is successfully transitioned to the production environment. 
  • Responds to support and change requests as business needs evolve over time.
  • Supports all company technology standards to ensure reliable operations.
  • Maintains strategic relationships with suppliers and/or technologies that meet company needs and standards.
  • Prepares accurate statements of work and financial justification analysis.

Job Requirements

  • Master's degree in Operations Research, Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Computer Science, or a related field
  • Ph.D. in Operations Research, Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Computer Science, or a related field preferred
  • 5+ years building models and developing algorithms for mathematical programming
  • 5+ years researching and building optimization based solutions
  • 2+ years object oriented programming (C++ preferred)
  • 2+ years utilizing commercial optimization solvers (e.g. CPLEX, DASH, Gurobi) and software profiling and memory management tools
  • 5+ years industry experience with demonstrated business impact in reducing logistics or resource scheduling costs through the application of optimization models preferred
  • 5+ years industry experience designing/building/testing optimization models forecasting systems and business metrics preferred
  • Experience teaching and mentoring other scientists and software engineers preferred
  • Experience in prototyping and developing software in the Java programming language
  • Experience in multi-process, multi-threaded optimization implementations
  • Demonstrated knowledge in the following:  theories of optimization including linear programming, mixed-integer programming and network modeling
  • Strong communication skills, to include verbal, written, and presentation, for interaction with senior management and colleagues, as well as cross functional teams
  • Demonstrates system engineering/analytical/problem solving skills
