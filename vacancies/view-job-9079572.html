Job Description:

The Academic Educational Technologist will act as tier two support for Blackboard Learn, Blackboard Collaborate, Kaltura, and other applications supported by UMIT's Learning Platforms Team. The academic educational technologist will also assist in managing supported applications, discovering and evaluating new and updated software and extensions to existing systems, managing the deployment of new software and utilities, integrating new technologies with existing systems, troubleshooting system problems, documenting internal departmental procedures and end-user workflows. The successful applicant should be self-motivated to learn new skills, well organized, have excellent communication and troubleshooting skills, and be excited about trying out new technologies to enhance teaching and learning.



Primary Duties and Responsibilities:
* Act as tier 2 support for applications supported by UMIT's Learning Platforms Team
* Troubleshoot issues with supported systems and applications
* Create training materials to accommodate the self-learners and provide resources for our "customers"
* Design, Implement, and Document workflows for supported applications
* Assist in managing supported systems and applications
* Assist in integrating new technologies into existing systems using feeds and APIs
* Discover, explore, and evaluate new applications to enhance teaching and learning
* Develop or modify code to enhance features
* Other duties as assigned



Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities
* Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer service
* The ability to write technical documentation for end users
* Skills in managing one's own time and the time of others
* The ability to communicate information and ideas in speaking/writing so others will understand
* Strong attention to detail, time/project management and organization skills
* Working knowledge of computer programming
* Ability to troubleshoot technical issues


Education and Work Experience Requirements:
* Bachelor's degree and at least two years' experience in information technology or any appropriate combination of relevant education, certifications and/or work experience will be considered
* Experience programming with one or more high-level programming languages (Python, Java, C++, etc.)



Desired Skills
* Experience supporting Blackboard Learn or a similar Learning Management System
* Experience writing technical documentation for non-technical users
* Experience supporting Windows and macOS
* Experience supporting web applications
* Experience writing SQL queries
* Experience with systems integration
* Working knowledge of the Linux command line
* Experience with programming in multiple computer languages
