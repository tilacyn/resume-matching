GENERAL SUMMARY/ OVERVIEW STATEMENT:


The MGPO Physician Analytics & Business Intelligence team within the MGPO Finance division is perceived as one of the more advanced and robust analytic groups within the Partners Healthcare umbrella. This team is responsible for managing the platform known as IPORT (Interactive Performance Optimization Reporting Tool) which combines administrative, financial and clinical data sets in a data warehouse and provides the ability to view drivers in volume and reimbursement trends tied to the revenue cycle through comprehensive reports, scorecard capabilities, interactive dashboards and ad hoc reporting tools.

As a Sr. Technical Business Analyst, you will be a senior member of a of highly technical team (including analysts, architects and developers) who are responsible for enterprise wide data platforms. The successful candidate will positively contribute in the day-to-day responsibilities of the team by documenting Business and Technical Requirements.

S/he will work closely with data warehouse leadership to ensure that appropriate technical and analytical solutions are provided and to meet desired goals.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:


Partner with stakeholders to listen, understand and document their needs

Creates business and technical specifications for database, reporting and analytics that meet business needs

Provide consistency in documentation approach, using accepted methodologies and templates.

Develop technical work-flow, prototypes, and guidelines that meet business needs.

Turn business requirements into technical specifications including database models and technical requirements documents.

Regularly reviews and proposes updates to current standards and processes to align them with changing organizational policy and procedures or with industry best practices.

Enhances, maintains and supports internal systems, software and tools utilized by the team, including, but not limited to, version control, change management, and support ticketing.

Able to move large amounts of data in files or within database tables using common data transfer or ETL tools such as Microsoft SSIS/SSDT.

Independently plans and prepares for and manages deployments into production environment by following team's established standards and processes.

Coordinates and schedules system updates, patches, and configuration changes.
Able to communicate and coordinate effectively with other team members as well as end users.

Manages and maintains relationships with vendors and teams from other parts of the organization to provide seamless support to end users on the systems he or she is responsible for.

Communicates technical/complex information well both verbally and in writing. Answers technical queries and assists team members and users whenever needed.

Able to establish and maintain cooperation, understanding, trust and credibility.

Able to perform multiple tasks concurrently and effectively.

Maintains technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing publications; establishing personal networks; participating in technical societies.

Maintains client and user confidence and protects operations by keeping sensitive information confidential.
