Job Description:

Job Summary



  • As our Solution Architect (Remote), you will facilitate the successful implementation of our Client's software with their customers, increase product adoption, and share best practices in data governance.


A DAY IN THE LIFE OF A SOLUTION ARCHITECT:
You'll be reporting directly to the Professional Services Manager, and will be responsible for a wide range of tasks, including:



  • Responsible for writing statement of work (SOW) that include resource types and duration.

  • Develop plans and manage client sponsor relationships including status report and change requested.

  • Implement metadata, business glossary and governance and MDM solutions.

  • Develop relational databases and java based web application.

  • Create Data Governance Center (DGC) models including assets, relationships, domains and communities.

  • Define workflow, security and integration requirements as well as define dashboard and reporting requirements.

  • Define technical architecture including sizing development, QA, production environments and disaster recovery.

  • Write functional specifications, technical specifications and test scripts.

  • Configure DGC based on client requirements and specifications.

  • Document case studies and create solution templates that will accelerate future implementations.

  • Lead workshops and requirements gathering activities as well as provide recommendations and best practices for organizational change management, roles and training plans.

  • Participate in community forums and blogs. Present at industry events and tradeshows and assist with mentoring Company service partners.


Qualifications
Education



  • Bachelor's degree or foreign equivalent in Computer Science, Management of Technology, Computer Engineering, or a related field


Experience



  • 5+ years of progressively responsible experience in the job offered, or as a Senior Data Governance Analyst, Data Governance Analyst, Software Associate, or a related position. Open to suitable combination of education, training, or experience

  • 5+years of progressively responsible experience in the following areas:

  • Enterprise Architecture

  • Experience working in complex enterprise software implementation projects.

  • Relational Database Management Systems (SQL Server, MySQL, or Oracle)

  • Scoping and writing SOWs (highly preferred)

  • BI Tools like Tableau (preferred)

  • Mulesoft Certified (highly preferred)

  • Must have two (2) years working experience with Data Governance Center (DGC) or equivalent Data Governance or Data Management technologies. Alternatively, the candidate can also have a minimum of two (2) years working knowledge of ESB and EAI technologies, preferably Mulesoft.


Skills



  • Excellent written and verbal English language skills with sound communication and presentation skills.

  • The appetite to work in a fast-paced, high-growth company
