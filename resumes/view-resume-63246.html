To whom it may concern;
I thoroughly understand the critical business needs and requirements of complete and accurate technical communication. This initial information demonstrates that understanding and presents my attitude and philosophy of work that I bring to the benefit of your company.
An outstanding writer always keeps focus and attention on three factors of a documentation project:
 Thorough knowledge of the target audience(s)
 Detailed documentation plan
 Efficient content collection processes
Thorough Knowledge of the Target Audience(s)
The real secret to writing effectively comes down to this reality  when the audience information needs are known, the creation of effective content is easy. A thorough profile of each audience is done once, with updates every two to five years.
Necessary details include language comprehension, technical expertise, work environment, how, why & when a document is accessed, and preferred presentation format (words, graphics, audio, video, etc.)
Detailed Documentation Planning
Companies with effective documentation plans are able to start any project with a template. (Companies without plan templates need to hire me immediately.) The key features are: resources (documents, target audience profiles, SMEs, project document outlines and the all-important schedules.
Efficient Content Collection Processes
It is my preference to collect material as early in the development process as possible. From this a detailed outline is generated. Previous material is retained, placeholders for new material is positioned. As much as possible, initial drafts are created from internal docs and on testing machines. This reduces the need to interact with SMEs to straight-forward topic review and corrections  all done by email and without any deadline pressures on anyone.
Personal Attitude
I am a pleasant, self-effacing individual who is pro-actively develops and maintains clean communication lines throughout the entire group. When requested, I will fit my schedule to the availability of SMEs.
My personal satisfaction is based on the absence of problems, confusions, or misunderstandings by the target audience (and within the project group). My sole personal metric is based on customer satisfaction of the documentation.
I did not choose this career for the kudos - no one thinks to actually thank the writer. I take great pride in assembling words to precisely communicate the necessary information for the reader.
I am an individual who loves to arrive early at work, get the coffee started, and start setting up my work day. I use a sense of humor and previously negotiated agreements to ensure smooth and painless interactions. My information collection efforts are designed for the least possible interruption of schedules.
