suicidal event, where God came into my
life and altered it

completely. From this intimate encounter
with God that night, I saught to believe
in and follow into a deep intimate
relationship with

Him; thus enabling me to mature in my
faith and to seek Him through all of my
lifes great events. Through His healing
hand I was able

to conquer my depression and speak out

Ministry Statement

I desire to teach the youth about why
they were created, who created them, and
how that loving act continues in their
life and in the

world. I want them to understand who the
Trinity is and why God created His
creation. I want them to understand the
fall and the

beginning of sin and how that still
plays in their life. I want them to
understand how God, in His love and
sacrifice, was able to

reconcile his creation back into a
relationship with him and, in Jesus
sacrifice, crucifixion, and
resurrection, they are now able to

have an intimate relationship with Him.
I want them to be encountered by God
everyday and be encouraged to spread the
news of the

Gospel to everyone in their lives. I
want them to be able to see their lives
through the lens of their faith and have
a dynamic

relationship with God that continues to
transform and renew them and everyone
they encounter in the world. In
addition, I would desire

to add into the ministry is a practical
artistic methodology of their faith that
build on theological principles and
challenges each

student to delve deeper into their own
relationship with God. I desire to add
art into the curriculm so they can begin
to create a

piece that they will create on their own
that will be a symbol that they can look
throughout their journey. This pieces
will be

designed to help teach students simple
artistic practices that will enable them
to create meaningful artwork based off
the lessons that

are being taught that they can begin to
share with the rest of the group and be
encouraged to dialogue with the group
about what they

have created and what impact it has on
their faith and how they see God working

another aim is

to teach the students to understand how

faith rather than elements that go
