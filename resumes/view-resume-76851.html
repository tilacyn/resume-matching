Objective:
Information Technology Internship Program
We are one of the fastest growing IT services company in the DC Metro area. We're looking for students who are currently working towards a bachelor's or master's degree in computer science, management information systems, or a related technical or business field, for our online internships. These interns will be considered for our full time positions once they complete the requisite number of internships as follows -
Sophomores- 25-35 weeks (350-487 hrs)
Juniors  15-25 weeks (211-350 hrs)
Seniors  6-15 weeks (84-211 hrs)
Our Information Technology Internship Program provides highly motivated and qualified students an opportunity to gain first-hand experience building applications using leading-edge systems and tools while they are still at school and receive valuable support from our Mentors. Our interns are viewed as a key source of future talent for entry-level IT careers within the company and by our clients.
Program components include
 Perform Requirements gathering, Analysis and Design
 Code and create the architecture for the application selected
 Create technical documentation (SRS, BRD) and submit them to their Mentors
 Write software programs in n-Tiered Architecture  front end, back end and the business layer
 Creating technical specifications and test plans;
 Work closely with the experienced Mentors
 Perform Unit Testing of the application built before deploying

Application tools used during internship include, but are not limited to
 Eclipse
 Weblogic
 JBoss
 Visual Studio
 Selenium
 Azure
 SQL server
 Oracle Database
 Unix
